Overview

The Volvo FH16 is one of the most powerful and prestigious heavy-duty trucks in the world. Designed for long-haul transportation and demanding applications, it combines exceptional power, driver comfort, and advanced safety features.

The FH16 has become a symbol of premium quality in the commercial vehicle segment, trusted by professional drivers worldwide for its reliability and performance.

Technical Specifications

  • Engine: D16 16-liter inline-6
  • Power: 550-750 HP
  • Torque: 2,800-3,550 Nm
  • Transmission: I-Shift 12-speed
  • GCW: Up to 325,000 lbs
  • Fuel Tank: 500 liters
  • Cab Options: Multiple heights
  • Year: 1993-Present

Interesting Facts

  • Can pull up to 325,000 lbs in certain configurations
  • Volvo invented the three-point seatbelt
  • I-Shift transmission handles up to 3,550 Nm of torque
  • Cab designed for 1.5 million km service life
  • Fuel efficiency improved 30% over 20 years
  • Popular in Europe, Australia, and Asia
