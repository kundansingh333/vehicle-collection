  3. Ducati Panigale V4

Overview

The Ducati Panigale V4 represents the pinnacle of Italian motorcycle engineering, combining MotoGP-derived technology with stunning design. It's the first mass-production Ducati to feature a four-cylinder engine, marking a significant departure from the company's traditional V-twin configuration.

This superbike delivers exceptional performance with 214 horsepower while maintaining the beautiful aesthetics Ducati is famous for. The bike features cutting-edge electronics and aerodynamics directly derived from racing.

Technical Specifications

  • Engine: 1,103cc V4
  • Power: 214 HP @ 13,000 rpm
  • Torque: 124 Nm @ 10,000 rpm
  • Transmission: 6-speed
  • Top Speed: 300 km/h
  • 0-60 mph: 2.6 seconds
  • Weight: 175 kg (dry)
  • Year: 2018-Present

Interesting Facts

  • V4 engine shares 90% components with MotoGP Desmosedici
  • Aerodynamic winglets add 66 lbs of downforce at 168 mph
  • Features color TFT dashboard with customizable modes
  • Named after Borgo Panigale, Ducati's manufacturing town
  • Dominated World Superbike Championship races
  • Titanium exhaust weighs only 19 pounds
