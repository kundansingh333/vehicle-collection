Overview

The Tesla Model S revolutionized the automotive industry by proving that electric vehicles could be luxurious, high-performing, and practical for daily use. It combined cutting-edge electric vehicle technology with software-driven features and over-the-air updates.

The Model S established a new paradigm for what a modern car could be, forcing traditional automakers to accelerate their electric vehicle programs.
