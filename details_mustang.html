Overview

The 1967 Ford Mustang represents the pinnacle of American muscle car engineering. This iconic vehicle revolutionized the automotive industry and created an entirely new segment of performance cars that combined style, power, and affordability.

The 1967 model year saw the Mustang grow slightly larger with a more aggressive stance. It featured a redesigned front end with a concave grille and larger taillights, making it instantly recognizable.

Technical Specifications

  • Engine: V8 7.0L
  • Horsepower: 335 HP
  • Torque: 440 lb-ft
  • Transmission: 4-speed manual
  • Top Speed: 210 km/h
  • 0-60 mph: 6.5 seconds
  • Weight: 1,361 kg
  • Year: 1967

Interesting Facts

  • Over 472,000 units sold in 1967 model year
  • Created the "pony car" class of vehicles
  • Featured in "Gone in 60 Seconds" as Eleanor
  • Available in 13 different exterior colors
  • Well-preserved models sell for over $100,000
