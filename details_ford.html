Overview

The Ford F-150 is America's best-selling truck for over 45 consecutive years, combining utility with modern comfort and technology. It represents the ultimate workhorse that can handle any job while providing car-like comfort and features.

The F-150 has evolved from a simple work truck to a sophisticated vehicle that serves as both a capable tool and a comfortable daily driver.

Technical Specifications

  • Engine Options: Multiple
  • Power: 250-700 HP
  • Torque: Up to 780 lb-ft
  • Transmission: 10-speed auto
  • Towing: Up to 14,000 lbs
  • Payload: Up to 3,325 lbs
  • Drive: 4WD/2WD options
  • Year: 1975-Present

Interesting Facts

  • Best-selling vehicle in America for 40+ years
  • Aluminum body saves up to 700 pounds
  • Raptor variant designed for high-speed off-road
  • Over 1 million F-Series trucks sold annually
  • Available in Regular, SuperCab, and SuperCrew cabs
  • Hybrid PowerBoost offers 430 HP and 12,700 lb towing
