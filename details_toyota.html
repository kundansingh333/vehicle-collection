Overview

The Toyota Land Cruiser is legendary for its unmatched reliability, off-road capability, and longevity. It's one of the longest-running automotive nameplates, having been in continuous production for over 70 years.

The Land Cruiser has built a reputation as the vehicle that can go anywhere and last forever, trusted by the United Nations and explorers worldwide.

Technical Specifications (300 Series)

  • Engine: 3.5L V6 twin-turbo
  • Power: 409 HP @ 5,200 rpm
  • Torque: 479 lb-ft
  • Transmission: 10-speed auto
  • Drivetrain: Full-time 4WD
  • Towing: 8,000 lbs
  • Ground Clearance: 8.9 inches
  • Year: 1951-Present

Interesting Facts

  • Sold in over 170 countries - more than any other Toyota
  • Over 10 million Land Cruisers sold globally
  • Commonly exceed 300,000 miles
  • Australian outback operators often exceed 500,000 miles
  • BBC Top Gear called it "indestructible"
  • Vehicle of choice for UN in harsh environments
