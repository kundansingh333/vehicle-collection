Overview

The Harley-Davidson Sportster is an iconic American motorcycle that has been in continuous production for over six decades. It represents the quintessential American cruiser experience with its distinctive V-twin engine sound, classic styling, and rich heritage.

The Sportster has evolved significantly while maintaining its core character, making it one of the longest-running motorcycle models in history.

Technical Specifications (1200)

  • Engine: 1,202cc V-twin
  • Power: 67 HP @ 5,500 rpm
  • Torque: 73 lb-ft @ 4,000 rpm
  • Transmission: 5-speed
  • Top Speed: 190 km/h
  • Fuel Capacity: 12.5 liters
  • Weight: 258 kg (wet)
  • Year: 1957-Present
